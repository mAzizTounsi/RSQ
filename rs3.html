A concentrate of pure pleasure

With its sleek design, 400 hp five-cylinder engine and distinctive sound, the new Audi RS 3 Sportback transports you to an experience of rare intensity. Combined fuel consumption*: WLTP: 9.6-9.5 l/100km (NEDC: 8.5-8.5 l/100km) CO2 emissions*: WLTP: 216-214 g/km (NEDC: 195-194 g/km)

Equipped with a 2.5 TFSI engine, the new Audi RS 3 Sportback can reach a top speed of 280 km/h on the track. With 33 hp more power than the previous generation, giving it 400 hp, the supercharged compact car can sprint from 0 to 100 km/h in just 4.1 seconds. The unique 1-2-4-5-3 ignition sequence of the 5-cylinder engine gives you a distinctive sound and amplifies your experience on the road.

Lightning-fast gear changes and flawless agility in all conditions, the new Audi RS 3 Sportback inherits a 7-speed S tronic transmission and the legendary quattro permanent all-wheel drive. The RS electronic stabilization control has a Sport mode that puts you in control of any situation. With Audi drive select you can adapt your driving style to your mood of the day.

Intensity from all angles The new Audi RS 3 Sportback asserts its racing genes: Singleframe grille with honeycomb grille, quattro logo, large air intakes, profiled RS bumpers ... A the rear, roof spoiler, oval tailpipes and diffuser complete the dynamism of the new Audi RS 3 Sportback. Optional 19-inch aluminum rims and ceramic brakes are available.
